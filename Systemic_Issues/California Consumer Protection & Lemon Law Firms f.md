### Key Considerations for Your Case

Your situation presents strong claims for:

1. **Breach of Warranty Service Obligations** - Dealerships failed to perform repairs in a workmanlike manner and didn't follow manufacturer bulletins[^1]
2. **Service Negligence** - Multiple documented instances where dealerships ignored or improperly applied manufacturer directives (H291, H299, JLRTB02030)[^1]
3. **Song-Beverly Consumer Warranty Act (California Lemon Law)** - Recurring defects that substantially impair use, value, or safety, with multiple repair attempts[^20][^53]

Most California lemon law attorneys work on **contingency fee basis** with **no upfront costs**. Under California law, if you prevail, the manufacturer pays your attorney fees and costs.[^18][^50][^45]
