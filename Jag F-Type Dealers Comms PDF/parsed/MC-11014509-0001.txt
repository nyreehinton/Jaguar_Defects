SITUA TION
 The Augmented Reality Camera (ARCAM)
shows up blurry when activated. 
 
CAUSE
 Contamination on the inside of the windshield
in front of the Autonomous Driving Camera
(ADCAM; arrow).
 
 
CORRECTION
Using a clean and dry microfiber cloth, clean the af fected area of the inner windshield and ADCAM lenses
using BMW glass cleaner . 
 
PROCEDURE
1. Remove the ADCAM camera per the repair instructions listed in IST A/AIR 66 51 541 or 66 51 590.
