SUBJECT:  FRONT CAMERA SOFTWARE UPDATE FOR FCA & LKA LOGIC  
IMPROVEMENT  (SERVICE CAMPAIGN  TCF) 
This TSB supersedes 24-01-077H to revise the new ROM ID  to 1.08  on page 2.  
 
 
 
Description:  Certain Kona (SX2) vehicles may exhibit a warning light ON in the cluster related to the 
“Check Forward Safety system” due to software intended from an alternate region. A software update 
is available for the Front Camera system to update logic in the Forward Collision Avoidance (FCA) and 
Lane Keeping Assist (LKA) functions.  
 
Advanced Driver Assistance System (ADAS) functions are not affected due to this condition.   
 
 
                                                 
 
Applicable Vehicles  (Certain) : 
• 2025MY Kona (SX2)  produced 6/3/2024  – 7/19/2024

Dealers must perform this service campaign on all affected vehicles prior to customer retail delivery 
and whenever an affected vehicle is in the shop for any maintenance or repair.

ECU Update  Procedure
