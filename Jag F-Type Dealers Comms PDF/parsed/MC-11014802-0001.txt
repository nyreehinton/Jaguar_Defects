Subjec t: Replacing Fuel Tank Support Rods  
Make: FCCC 
Model: Tiffin XCR  
Model Year Affected: 2024  
Build Date Range: Selected Vehicles Built Between June 27, 2024 and June 27, 2024  
Work Instructions  
1.Park the vehicle, shut down the engine, and apply the parking brakes. Chock the tires.
2.Support the fuel tank cradle .
3.Remove the currently installed fuel tank support studs.
4. Install the correct studs, PN 03- 25287- 009, per drawing D03- 44520- 000. Install the upper end of the stud to the
fuel tank support on the chassis rail and place the washer and fastener on top.
5.Place
 the fastener and washer onto the stud , then insert them through the fuel tank cradle.
6.Place the washer and fastener at the bottom of the stud and tighten the fastener  118 lbf·ft ( 160 N ·m). Repeat th is
process at four places  for each fuel tank support stud.
