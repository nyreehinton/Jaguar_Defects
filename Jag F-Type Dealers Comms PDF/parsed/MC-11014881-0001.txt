   
SSM 53443   2024-2025 Nautilus - Direct Injection Fuel Injector Removal Or
Replacement
2024-2025 Nautilus vehicles require inspection and removal and discard of O-rings or debris that may be stuck
in the direct injection fuel rail injector bores when removing or replacing the injectors for any reason. Workshop
Manual (WSM), Section 303-04 Direct Fuel Injection Rail Removal and Installation has been updated to
address this condition.
