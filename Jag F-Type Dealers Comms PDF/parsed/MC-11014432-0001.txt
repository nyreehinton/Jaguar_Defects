TSB #     TSB 25-02 
 
Description/Title:   
Taillight harness grounding  
 
Affected Product Line(s):   
Certain model year 2025 Pasadena & Inception   
 
Service Campaign # :  RC000 323   
Code s: Inspect with Repair:  
TMCTS10101J  
  Document Date:  2/24/2025  
Time Allowed:   
Inspect with Repair:   
0.75  Document Revision:  A 
 
Issue:   
Part (s) Required:  
 
12”-24” 14 gauge white wire 
Heat shrink eyelet  
Heat shrink butt connector  
Wire nut  
 
Instructions:  
 
1. Find the rear taillight harness connector located inside of the driver chassis  rail. 
 
2. Expose the taillight harness  leading to the rear cap  and cut the 14-gauge  white wire near the 
chassis ground stud  near the hydraulic pump assembly .

3. Cap off the chassis side of the white wire.  It will no longer be needed.  
 
4. Connect the rear cap side of the cut white wire to a heat shrink butt connector.  
 
5. On the other end of the heat shrink butt connector, add a 14-gauge  white wire to reach the chassis 
ground stud with a heat shrink eyelet.  
 
6. Remove the chassis ground stud nut holding the hydraulic pump assembly ground and add the 
new taillight harness eyelet.  
 
7. Reinstall the nut to the chassis ground stud.  
 
8. Be sure to activate the heat shrink on the eyelet and butt connector.
