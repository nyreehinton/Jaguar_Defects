 
 
Page 1 of 2 Subject:  
The GEN 1 Husky 3 foam pump from FSC is being discontinued. To keep supporting our customers, a conversion 
kit is available to install the GEN 2.1  Husky 3 foam pump in place of the GEN 1.  
Purpose:   
To communicate with the Pierce Dealer/customer network that a conversion kit is available to replace the 
discontinued GEN 1 foam  pump when a replacement is needed.  
Description:   
The “old” GEN 1 Husky 3 foam pump ( PN: 2851584) uses a blue box signal conditioner for the position 
transducer in the foam pump, the “new” GEN 2.1  Husky 3  foam  pump ( PN: 4503618 ) uses a transducer that does 
not require a signal conditioner. (See Figure 1)
