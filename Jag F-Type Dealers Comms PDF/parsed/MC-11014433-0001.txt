SITUA TION
A Check Control message (CCM) displays for “T ransmission oil service required” (ID 2120). 
 
This warning will trigger based on the following that applies:
 
From 60,000 miles (~95,000 km) to 78,000 miles (~125,000 km), or
After six (6) years: The “six years” time interval is determined the production date of the vehicle. It is not
based on the day/date of the first registration, or the completion date of the pre-delivery check (PDI). 
 
If the vehicle is driven more than approx. 1900 miles (~3,000 km) after the first CCM, then a second
message will be display for “T ransmission fault!” (ID 368).
 
CAUSE
The oil in the double clutch transmission ages with increasing usage, as well as depending on the vehicle’ s
driving profile. 
 
When the transmission oil reaches the end of its service life, a regular "transmission oil change in the double
clutch transmission" is required.
 
CORRECTION
Change the oil in the DKG.
 
PROCEDURE
Follow  Repair Instruction 00 1 1 276 Oil change in the double-clutch transmission (DKG)
