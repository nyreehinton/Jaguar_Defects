SUBJECT   
Rear Axle Shock Absorber Incorrectly Installed  Bulletin No.:  TFTSB004  
Issue Date:  1/27/202 5 
 
APPLICABLE MODELS/VINS  
Model : MY 2024 TRE FCEV  
VINs:  R2393001  thru S2393247  
 
OVERVIEW  
Certain TRE FCEV vehicles may have had rear axle shock absorbers incorrectly installed.  Reinstalling the shock absorbers correctly 
will ensure proper vehicle performance.

PROCEDURE    
Preliminary Steps  
1. Inspect all four rear axle shock absorber s for incorrect installation.  
Note:  Incorrect installation is defined as the shock absorber’s protective tube located on the bottom and the reservoir tube on 
top when installed.  See Figure 1. 
 
 
Figure 1, Example of Shock Absorber Installed Incorrectly  
2. If a shock absorber  is found to be installed incorrectly , continue to Removal and Reinstallation  on page  3. 
3. If all shock absorber s are installed  correctly , the inspection is complete, and no further action is necessary.

PROCEDURE    
Removal and Reinstallation  
Note:  If a shock absorber  is found to be installed incorrectly, do the following procedure . See Figure 2 on page 4. 
1. Remove the nut  (1) from the upper bolt ( 2). 
2. Remove the upper bolt ( 2) from the shock absorber ( 5). 
3. Remove the nut  (3) from the lower bolt ( 4). 
4. Remove the lower bolt ( 4) from the shock absorber  (5). 
5. Remove the shock absorber  (5) from the vehicle.  
6. Position the shock absorber  (5) correctly into place on the vehicle.  
7. Install the lower bolt ( 4) to the shock absorber ( 5). 
8. Install the nut  (3) to the lower bolt ( 4). 
9. Install the upper bolt ( 2) to the shock absorber  (5). 
10. Install the nut  (1) to the upper bolt ( 2). 
11. Use a torque wrench to torque the two nuts (1, 2) to 169 ± 15 lb-ft (230.0 ± 20.0 N·m ). 
PROCEDURE IS COMPLETE
