PROCEDURE
The dif ferential cover of the reverser cannot be serviced by just replacing the sealing ring alone. This is
because a special device is required to properly assemble the eccentric shaft with the eccentric lever and
bring the lever system into the correct position.
The recommended repair solution is to replace the entire dif ferential cover of the reverser . 
Replacement dif ferential covers are now more readily available.

SITUATION 
 
Sealing ring on the differential cover of the reverser is leaking. 
 Radial shaft seal faulty. 
 
 
 
PROCEDURE 
 
It is not possible to change just the sealing ring in the differential cover of the reverser, as a special 
device is needed to assemble the eccentric shaft with the eccentric lever in order to bring the lever 
system into the correct position. The repair solution should be to replace the differential cover of the 
reverser, but these are currently unavailable.
