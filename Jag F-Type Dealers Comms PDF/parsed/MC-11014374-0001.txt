TECHNICAL SERVICE BULLETIN  
SERVICE ACTION: WINDSHIELD  
WASHER FLUID REPLACEMENT  (SA606) 
 
This bulletin provides  the procedure to replace the windshield wiper fluid on certain 2025MY  K4 (CL4) vehicles 
produced from January 20, 2025  to January 21, 2025, which may have been contaminated/mixed with engine 
coolant fluid during assembly.  The coolant fluid may form a n ‘oily/sticky ’ mixture preventing proper cleaning 
of the vehicle’s  windshield  when the windshield wiper is activated.  Follow the procedure outlined in this 
publication to  drain the existing  windshield washer fluid, rinse , and refill with new windshield washer fluid on 
the applicable vehicle . Before conducting the procedure, verify that the vehicle is included in the list of affected 
VINs  below .
