SPECIAL SERV ICE CAMPAIGN 24TC08  (Remedy Notice)  
 
Certain 2023 – 2024 Corolla HV Models  
Certain 2023 Prius Models  
Certain 2023 -2024 Prius Prime Models  
Fuel Tank Filler Clamp and Vapor Leak Detection  
 
Specific information for Region support is provided below.  
 
Condition  
In the subject vehicles, the vapor leak detection monitor system may not detect  a vapor leak from the fuel filler 
cap and illuminate the malfunction indicator lamp (MIL).   If fuel vapor leaks from the filler cap, it is possible 
that there could be a fuel smell.   
 
Dealer Notification  
The attached dealer letter will be sent to all Toyota dealers on January 30, 2025 . 
 
 
Please reference the attached Dealer Letter for additional details.  
 
Please review this notification with your staff to assure that all relevant personnel have been briefed regarding 
this subject.
