Reference SSM76252
Models New Range Rover / L460
New Range Rover Sport / L461
Title Deployable Side Steps - Noisy
Category Accessories
Last modified 28-Jan-2025 00:00:00
Symptom General Accessories
Content Model / Model Year / Derivative
Range Rover / 22MY onwards / Vehicles with deployable side steps
Range Rover Sport / 23MY onwards / Vehicles with deployable side steps
 
Situation:
JLR Engineering investigations have identified reports of noise from the deployable side steps even after
completion of the relevant TOPIx Cloud Workflows.
Cause:
The cause is under investigation. Sound files provided in support of this SSM will assist the ongoing Engineering
investigations.
 
Action:
Refer to the service request below.
Service Request:
Complete the relevant TOPIx Cloud Workflows which are available through 'Fault and Breakdown' by entering
the following symptoms:
Body > Accessory > Deployable Side Steps
Body > Accessory > Deployable Side Steps - Noise During Operation
Body > Accessory > Deployable Side Steps - Erratic/Intermittent Operation
Body > Accessory > Deployable Side Steps - Inoperative / Incorrect Operation
If there is still an abnormal noise from the deployable side steps after completing the relevant TOPIx Cloud
Workflows, continue below:
To assist Engineering with determining the root cause of certain noises, follow the below actions:
1. Raise an Electronic Product Quality Report (EPQR), which must include the below information:
- Reference to SVO_070 
- Reference to P394718
- Sound recording file(s) in .wav format to capture the abnormal noise from the deployable side steps
- Supporting notes, videos, photographs and observations
2. Update the EPQR to detail all completed actions.
(Ref 000394718 / 3580)
