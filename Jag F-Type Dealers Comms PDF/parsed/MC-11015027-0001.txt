SUBJECT: Aspen APU Shutdown, Engine Stall and Loss of Communication Alarms 
 
This bulletin is to bring awareness and provide troubleshooting for recent random Aspen APU unit shutdowns, 
engine stalls, and CAN alarms, 20001, 20002 and 20006. Consider these potential causes when performing 
troubleshooting.  
 Alternator circuit from B+ to starter positive.  
 Electrical harness from climate control module to Aspen Interface. 
 Main power cables at truck battery. (eyelet/terminal crimp over insulation)  
 Main buss bars for tight, clean connection with no broken eye terminals.  
 Inspect the APU connector and terminals at APU J1 for recessed sockets and or inadequate crimping. 
 Look over the engine harness for rubs against the APU frame, components and associated wires. 
 Check F2 Fuse. 
 Replace APU Module.
