DEFROSTER ADDITIONAL TEMPERATURE SENSOR  RETROFIT  
Retrofit Description:  
This procedure adds an additional temperature sensor to the defroster to prevent overheating.

Procedure  
1. Complete the Proterra approved Lockout/Tagout procedure to make the bus safe for work.  
 
2. Using a T-30 Torx Driver, uninstall all panels necessary to access the defroster.  
 
 
3. Remove the two orange wires from the Safety Thermostat shown in the following photograph. 
It is mounted next to the collar with the Low, Medium, and High temperature th ermostats.  
  
 
4. Using a #2 Phillips Screwdriver, remove both screws from the thermostat. Remove the thermostat. Retain the screws for use later.  Discard the thermostat.

8. Disconnect the connector  attached to the bottom relay that is mounted on the aluminum box 
on the side of the defroster  as shown in the following photograph. This is the connector with 
blue and orange wires.  
  
 
9. Using a Pin Extractor Tool, remove the center orange wire from the connector.  Place the 
section  of Heat Shrink that was supplied with the Harness ( 051767) over the orange wire and 
attached terminal . 
 
 
10. Connect the orange wire with the male terminal from the Harness ( 051767) to the terminal 
that was just removed from the connector. Cover the connection with the Heat Shrink. Apply 
heat to seal the connection.

16. Using Cable Ties ( 001912), secure the harness and other wiring.  
 
17. Using Side Cutting Pliers, trim the excess from the Cable Ties (001912).  
 
18. Using a T -30 Torx Driver, re install the panels that were removed earlier to access the 
defroster.  
 
19. Remove the Lockout/Tagout devices and power on the bus.  
 
20. Verify proper operation of the defroster on all speeds and heat settings before returning the 
bus to service.
