Campaign Bulletin: This campaign bulletin addresses a non -safety -related condition and provides recommended technical 
diagnosis and repair procedures. Apply this procedure to the applicable vehicles.  
Condition  
On certain Cybertruck vehicles, the left body controller's printed circuit board was misaligned with its heatsink, which 
may cause the board to crack when the enclosure is closed . This may lead to a graceful power -off event.  
Correction  
Replace the left  body  controller.  
 NOTE:  If this bulletin is applicable to the vehicle, perform the correction without any need for inspection.
