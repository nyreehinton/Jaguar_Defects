A new electrical powertrain ERAD / TVDC has been launched in model year 2025.
During the first year of production of this new ERAD, we need to be aware of quality issues as quickly 
as possible.
For this reason, CMQ (Volvo Cars Central Quality Organization) must be contacted before performing 
any repairs on the ERAD/TVDC unit.
No ERAD/TVDC units shall be opened or replaced without approval or request from the CMQ.
