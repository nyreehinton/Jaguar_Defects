SUBJECT:  IN-CABIN CAMERA ( ICC) LOGIC UPDATE  
(SERVICE CAMPAIGN  TCR) 
 
 
 
Description:  Certain 2024MY Santa Fe (MX5A) vehicles  may experience excessive alerts for In-Cabin 
Camera (ICC) blockage due to driving with hand (s) at the 12 o’clock position.  Updating the ICC  logic 
reduces the alerts . 
 
Applicable Vehicles  (Certain) : 
• 2024MY Santa Fe (MX5A) produced from 12 /28/2023–10/10/2024
