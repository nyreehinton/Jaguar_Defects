Symptom/Vehicle Issue :  Alarm Siren Does Not Function   
Customer  Complaint/T echnician  Observation:  The owner complains the vehicles siren is 
not working. The technician observes the vehicles Body Control Module (BCM) sets Diagnostic 
Trouble Code (DTC) U1412 -09 Alarm Siren Unit (ASU) Component Failure.  
 
Repair Procedure : An improperly seated connector may create a concern with the siren operation 
and set the above DTC. Inspect the involved connector at the siren, disconnect and reconnect and 
secure the connector at the siren. Ensure fuse F090 at the Body Control Module (BCM) is securely in 
place and retest the operation. If the DTC remains active continue with the service diagnostics within 
Service Library (SL) to complete Fig 1 and 2.
