Supplying spare or replacement keys is an important service for our customers. This quick reference chart will assist you 
in providing the correct keys. 
Before placing your order, refer to these procedures:
●PRMS  orders  (Parts Support -> Technical Parts Request)  will only be accepted for pre-1981 vehicles
(Vehicles without the 17 digit VIN).
●Please reference “Bulletin Group 8 D4” and the part number in the PRMS ticket.
Keys (cut or blank) are not available for alarm systems with separate switches.
