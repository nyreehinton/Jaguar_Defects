Title Washer Fluid Leak Concen - 25MY Defender OCTA
Category Body
Last modified 29-Jan-2025 00:00:00
Symptom 102000 Window/Glass
Content Issue:
Jaguar Land Rover are investigating a washer fluid leak concern on 25MY L663 OCTA vehicles.
Cause:
Under Investigation
Action:
The washer fluid leak is only detectable when filling the washer fluid reservoir to the maximum level.
During the Pre-Delivery inspection (PDI) please ensure that the washer fluid reservoir is filled to the maximum
level.
Once filled, check for any fluid leaks as instructed in the PDI manual and investigate any leaks fully before
releasing the vehicle to the client.
