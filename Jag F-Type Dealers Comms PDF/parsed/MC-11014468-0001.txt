Torsion bar cap screw kit 991860747 1 YesTorsion Bar Frame Plate Torque Value
Units Affected:  Certain AT37/40G aerial units mounted on 60” CA chassis built from August 2017 to 
September 2018. Verify your unit is affected by reviewing the attached list or accessing Altec Connect.
Background: Altec has learned that the torsion bar frame plates cap screws could be torqued to the in -
correct value on the affected units. This could cause premature wear on the cap screws. 
Customer Action:  Order and install the Torsion Bar Cap Screw Kit, part number 991860747, or sched -
ule the installation of this kit by Altec, by the next preventive maintenance cycle or within 60 days of 
receipt of this notice, whichever comes first. Completing this work will help to prevent premature wear on 
the torsion bar cap screws. Warranty for this repair expires February 25, 2027.
Subsequent damage due to failure to perform the required action(s) in the time period allowed will not be 
covered by warranty.
Ask your service provider to check for any outstanding notices at your next appointment.
Requirements:  The repair is estimated to take 2 hours and 1 person to complete. 
Completion and Warranty: The repair is covered under the Altec Warranty Policy until February 25, 
2027 and can be performed by Altec, the customer, or the customer’s warranty provider. Altec will per -
form the work for free at an Altec facility. If the customer or the customer’s warranty provider performs 
the work, a warranty claim must be submitted to be reimbursed for the cost of the parts and/or labor. Altec 
will allow up to $180.00 for the labor to perform the repair. Customers are responsible for the travel costs 
of an Altec Mobile Service technician if the technician performs the work at the owner’s location.
