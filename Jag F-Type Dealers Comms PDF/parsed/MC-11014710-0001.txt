Introduction    
Mack  Trucks has determined that a select group of trucks were built with an AHI Module 
fuel pressure sensor which does not meet our specifications. A sensor that falls outside 
of specifications may  result in diagno stic trouble codes  (DTCs ) and improper operation  of 
the aftertreatment system. Please follow the instruction in th is document to install an 
updated AHI fuel pressure sensor.
