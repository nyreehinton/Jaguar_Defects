Technical Journal 37159.2.0SERVICE:
The intention with this Technical Journal is to avoid fault tracing or part replacements for known 
deviations.
Following features are currently not fully activated/available: 
• Park Pilot Assist
• Double lock
• Automatically lock car when leaving
• Centre display in light mode (only Dark mode active)
• Wireless Apple CarPlay 
• Turn Off the Display to clean the center display
• Approach light, rear lamps not active
• Bluetooth might not connect automatically
• Pilot Assist (Hand detection together with active heated steering wheel)
Following features might need manual activation first time after central computer restart (once a 
night): 
• Approach “front light” 
• WPC (Wireless Phone Charging)
• Climate timer (Volvo Cars App can be used)
• Bluetooth connection
Following features are currently not planned to be available for EX90: 
• Light in steering wheel buttons
• Folding outer mirrors when locking the car 
  
Warranty claim info:
No warranty claim is accepted for this TJ.
