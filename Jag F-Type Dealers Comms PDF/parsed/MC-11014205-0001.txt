CONDITION   
Some 202 1-2025 MY Highlander HV , Sienna HV , RAV4, and RAV4 HV, may exhibit an oil leak from 
the area where the camshaft  cover, camshaft housing, and the timing chain  covers  meet. This is 
typically seen on the exhaust side of the engine.  
 
 
 
 
RECOMMENDATIONS   
If the condition is identified, and no previous related repairs have been made, please halt diagnostics 
and create a TAS case. If on a weekend or holiday, please proceed with repair and contact TAS on 
the next available business day.
