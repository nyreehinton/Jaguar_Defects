Defective Reverse Actuator
MODEL YEAR MODEL ENGINE
MY24 Spyder RT and F3 All
IMPORTANT
When receiving a vehicle for service:
• Always lookup the vehicle identification number (VIN) on the Knowledge Center to ensure all required recalls are performed.
• Always connect the vehicle to the BRP diagnostic software (BUDS) to ensure all required updates are performed.
PROBLEM
Reverse engagement may not be possible due to an internal
soldering problem that may cause premature failure of the reverse
actuator.
SOLUTION
Replace the reverse actuator of the affected vehicles.
REQUIRED PARTS
Order parts through the regular channel.

2. On all models, disconnect the reverse actuator connector.
3. Remove and keep the reverse actuator mounting screws and
nuts.
4. Detach the actuator cable from the control lever.
5. Using a suitable drill bit, Remove the cable from the factory
actuator by drilling the rivet head.
 NOTE
Holding the rivet head in a vise may help when drilling.
6. Install the cable on the new actuator and secure it using the
pop-rivet.
7. Install the actuator cable on the control lever.  DEFECTIVE REVERSE ACTUATOR
