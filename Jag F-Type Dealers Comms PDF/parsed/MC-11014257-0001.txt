FORTY FOOT COMPRESSOR SNOKEL INTAKE  RETROFIT  
Description  
The procedure adds a snorkel to the input of the air compressor to reduce contamination of the 
compressor.

Procedure  
 
1. Complete the Proterra approved Lockout/Tagout procedure to make the bus safe for work.  
 
2. This procedure will remove the Compressor Air Intake shown in the first illustration and extend 
it with a Snorkel as shown in the second illustration.  
  
 
3. This procedure shows all steps for installing the Snorkel on a 40- foot bus with Aux Heat. This 
represents the most complex installation. Some steps may be omitted if the Compressor Air Intake is more easily accessible than it is on the bus with Aux Heat shown in this procedure.  
 
4. Open the Trunk Hatch and the Curbside Rear hatch to access the Air Compressor.  
 
 
5. Inspect the bus to determine if the Air Compressor Intake Worm Drive Clamp can be loosened with the Compressor Air Filter  Assembly installed.  
  
6. If the Intake Worm Drive Clamp is accessible, proceed to step 14 . Otherwise proceed to step 7
