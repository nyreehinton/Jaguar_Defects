Correction:
1) Inspect the bare ground wire at connector X225 for the bare wire touching the Phase A
or B terminals. If the bare wire is touching, reroute the wire awa y from the Phase A and B
terminals and isolate with electrical tape if needed.
2) Inspect ground wire cutting into Phase A and/or Phase B wire. If the bare wire is
touching, reroute wire in harness and isolate with electrical tape and repair the wire
damage to the Phase A and/or Phase B wires
 
3) If you perform either repair above,  push the reset button in the X81 Pick up box
powerpack outlets and cycle the ignition and retest system.
