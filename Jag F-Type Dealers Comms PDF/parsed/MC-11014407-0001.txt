Markets: North American markets only
Issue: Some of the vehicles listed in the Model statement above may exhibit an illuminated MIL with DTC P20A5
stored in the PCM. This may be due to the software in the GDM and does not affect vehicle operation. Due to OBD
functionality, the GDM may be referred to as the SOBDMC in the FDRS scan tool.
Action: For vehicles that meet all of the criteria in the Issue and Model statements, follow the Service Procedure to
reprogram the SOBDMC.
Warranty Status: Eligible under provisions of New Vehicle Limited Warranty (NVLW)/Emissions Warranty/Service
Part Warranty (SPW)/Service Part New Vehicle (SPNV)/Extended Service Plan (ESP) coverage. Limits/policies/prior
approvals are not altered by a TSB. NVLW/Emissions Warranty/SPW/SPNV/ESP coverage limits are determined by
the identified causal part and verified using the OASIS part coverage tool.
