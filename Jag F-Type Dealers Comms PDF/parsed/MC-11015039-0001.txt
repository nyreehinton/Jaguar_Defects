Symptom/Vehicle Issue : Steering Wheel Off Center . Diagnostic Trouble Code (DTC) 
C0051 -00 Set. 
 
 
Discussion : If the steering wheel is off center with DTC C0051 -00 STEERING WHEEL 
POSITION SENSOR (MODULE, Brake System Control (BSCM))  perform the following 
steps . 
 
1. Place the car in the alignment rack, make sure the wheels are straight.  
2. Use WiTECH to r ead DTC (s) and document them on the repair order.  
3. Navigate to the electric power steering module (EPS) . Locate the stee ring angle 
sensor degrees in Data. Record the reading on the repair order.  
4. Under Misc F unctions, perform Steering Angle Calibration ro utine.  
5. In the brake system control module (BSCM) under Misc Functions, perform 
Reset Steering Angle Sensor Offset routine.  
6. C0051 -00 should now be stored. If so, clear all DTC(s)  
7. Perform ignition cycle and verify the DTC C0051 -00 is no longer present.  
8. Perform Test Drive and verify the Steering wheel is centered.  
9. Verify the DTC is no longer present.
