 
 
SERVICE ADVISORY # 25-460 
Coleman Water Heater Switch 
It has been determined that the water heater switch may be wired incorrectly. The following 
procedure describes how to identify and repair water heater wiring. 
Models Included: 2025 Coleman 13B Travel Trailer  
Serial Number Range : SH939648 - SH940999  
Note – Check serial number on Key Express to verify the campaign is open. Some vehicles were 
repaired before they left Keystone. 
Parts Required:  1 – KRV # 305662- Switch - Dummy/Hole Plug - Monitor Panel - .55" x 1.125" 
– Medium ( This part may be ordered in bulk without a VIN ) 
2 – KRV # 267572-Terminal - Bell Cap - Insulated - 16-10 Ga - CE551R 
Tools Required:   Screw gun with #2 square bit - Wire crimpers 
When performing warranty or recall service, please make certain that appropriate Personal 
Protective Equipment (PPE) is used. 
The remedy is to repair wiring at the switch. Red wire to top of switch and Green/White wires on bottom of 
the switch. 
REPAIR INSTRUCTIONS  
Step 1 
Locate the trailer on a level, flat, 
hard surface. Chock the wheels and 
disconnect all power. 
Step 2 
Remove water heater switch and 
inspect the wiring, see Figures 1 and 
2. If the wiring is incorrect, proceed to 
Step 4 to repair wiring.

Step 3 
If wiring is correct, proceed to warranty reimbursement. If the

Step 4 
To repair wiring, ensure red wire is on the top post of the switch. If a red jumper is present, cap it off 
with a bell cap. Both green/white wires go together on the bottom of the switch. Red/black wire and 
the two white wires should be capped together as shown in Fig 3. Discard red light and install switch 
blank. Proceed with warranty reimbursement.
