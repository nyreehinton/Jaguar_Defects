Symptom/Vehicle Issue : P1CDE -00 - Hybrid/EV Wake -Up Control 2 Output Circuit 
Short To Batter y Set In The B attery Pack Control Module (BPCM)  
 
Discussion:  The customer may bring their vehicle in for service because the  Red 
Wrench Service Electrical System lamp is illuminated  in the cluster. Upon scanni ng the 
vehicle  for DTCs you may find P1CDE -00 - Hybrid/EV Wake -Up Control 2 Output 
Circuit Short To Battery set in the Battery Pack Control Module (BPCM) . 
 
P1CDE -00 should be ig nored and there will be no effect  on vehicle operation or battery 
performance . Engineering is developing a BPCM software up date to change the 
internal diagnostics that are setting this fault. This software update will be released in 
the form of a TSB or RSU in late Q1 2025.
