Case Number : S2123000084  Rev. A  
 
Release Date:  February 2025  
 
Symptom/Vehicle Issue :  1st Row Seat Belt Retractor D -Ring Is Binding And Not 
Able To Rotate  
 
Diagnosis : The customer may find that the 1st row seatbelt is binding and not able to 
rotate freely. The issue  may be caused by  the nut for the Adjustable Turning L oop ATL 
(commonly referred to  as the D -Ring).  
 
 
Repair Procedure:  You will need to replace the D-Ring retaining nut, Part #:  
06509460AA.   
 
1. Remove the appropriate upper B -pillar trim and position aside.  Follow Removal 
instructions in Service Library.  
 
2. Remove the front seat belt turning loop nut from the front seat belt adjustable 
turning loop (ATL).  
 
3. Rotate D -ring on ATL stud to ensure D -ring can rotate  
 
4. Obtain  a new nut. Place the new replacement nut on the stud and tighten down 
using torque specifications provided in Service Library.  
 
5.  Re-install upper B -pillar trim . Follow Instillation instructions in Service Library.  
 
Verification:  Verify the D -ring can now rotate freely.
