Reference SSM76256
Models Discovery / L462
Title Accessory `Fixed Side Step` - Brackets Cracked
Category Body
Last modified 28-Jan-2025 00:00:00
Symptom 108000 Ext. Trim/Accessories
Content Model / Model Year / Derivative
Discovery / 21MY onwards / All
 
Situation:
JLR Engineering investigations have identified reports of front brackets for accessory 'Fixed Side Steps' being
installed without the spacers. This causes the brackets to fracture during installation.
The spacers are required when installing the 'Fixed Side Steps' on 21MY onwards vehicles and are detailed in
the TOPIx installation instructions.
 
Action:
Refer to the service information below.
Service Information:
When installing accessory 'Fixed Side Steps' VPLRP0347 and VPLRP0530 on 21MY onwards vehicles, follow
the TOPIx installation instructions - Refer to workshop manual section:
102-03 - Exterior - Fixed Side Steps - VPLRP0269/VPLRP0347 (G2738368) - REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION.
Spacer kit 'VPLRP0487' must be installed on the front brackets as shown in step 5 of the installation instructions.
