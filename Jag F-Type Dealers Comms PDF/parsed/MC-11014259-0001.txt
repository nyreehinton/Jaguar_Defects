Service Procedure:  
1. Turn the unit off using the HMI.  
2. Open the front access door and turn the engine ON/OFF switch to the OFF position.  
3. Disconnect battery power and all external power sources to the APU unit.  
4. Remove the front access cover from the unit.  
5. Clean the fuel pump area.

 6. Disconnect the fuel pump harness connection.  
7. Remove the fuel line and fitting from the left -hand side of the fuel pump. Cap the fuel line to 
prevent debris contamination. Make note of fuel flow arrow on the pump.  
8. Remove the three (3) fasteners that secure the fuel pump and strainer to the unit.  
9. Pull the fuel pump and strainer slightly outwards to allow pump removal.  
IMPORTANT:  DO NOT pull excessively on the assembly to prevent damage to the fuel line 
connected to the back the strainer.  
10. Secure the 90- degree strainer fitting in position with plyers or vice- lock plyers. Unscrew the 
pump from the 90- degree strainer fitting.  
11. Remove the fuel pump from the unit and clean any e xcess  fuel. Dispose of the pump 
according to local regulations . 
12. Fuel Pump Kit 401525  you will only use the fuel pump and discard other items in kit.  
13. Cut off existing connector at connector leaving wires as long as possible.  
14. Strip red and black wires on fuel pump for new terminal pins . 
15. Install new terminal pins 425396 qty 2 (ordered separately) and crimp onto fuel pump wires. 
Use crimping tool part number 640160133 available online through outside vendors.  
16. Install red wire into Pin 1 on connector  and install black wire into Pin 2 on connector.  
 
17. Clean the threads of the 90- degree fitting on the strainer. Apply thread sealing or locking 
compound to the threads of the 90- degree fitting.  
18. Make note of fuel flow direction arrow on the new fuel pump. The arrow must be pointing 
away from the strainer when installed.

 19. Secure the 90- degree strainer fitting in position with plyers or vice- lock plyers. Thread the 
fuel pump inlet port onto the 90- degree fitting. Tighten the fuel pump 2- 3 revolutions after 
initial finger -tight installation to ensure proper mounting orientation and prevent fuel leaks.   
20. Place the fuel pump and strainer assembly into its mounting location s. Install and torque the 
three (3) mounting fasteners to specification:  
 Two Fuel Pump Screws:  63 in -lbs (7 Nm)  
 Single strainer Screw:      22 in -lbs (2.5 Nm)  
21. Attach the fuel line and fitting to the left -hand side of the fuel pump. Tighten the fitting 2- 3 
revolutions after initial finger -tight installation to ensure proper sealing and prevent fuel leaks.  
22. Reconnect the fuel pump harness connection.  Secure fuel pump connector and harness with 
bandwrap to harness as shown below.
