SITUA TION
Groaning noise in the front parcel shelf of the luggage compartment area. The noise is mostly head when
the vehicle interior is cold.
 
CAUSE
The play of the retaining springs on the left and right of the front parcel shelf, is too large.
 
CORRECTION
Replace the front parcel shelf with part number 51 47 6 926 674, AI (revision index) 06 or higher .
 
PROCEDURE
1. Verify the customer ’s complaint.
2. If the noise is reproducible with the front parcel shelf installed, then the shelf will need to be
replaced with part number 51 47 6 926 674.
 
 The AI (revision index) of the new part must
be 06 or higher (circled).
 
 
3. Once the new front parcel shelf is installed, verify repair .
