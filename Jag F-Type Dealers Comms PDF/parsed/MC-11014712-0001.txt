Introduction  
Mack Trucks has determined that certain Mack sleeper cab assemblies were possibly 
manufactured with an insufficient number of welds across the rear flange of the upper 
header. The reduced number of welds could lower the  designed cab integrity . Follow the 
instructions below to inspect the header, drill, and add 30 M6 bolts with nuts if 
necessary.

Repair Procedure  
1. Park the vehicle on  a level surface .  
2. Apply parking brake .  
3. Place the transmission in park or neut ral. 
4. Turn off the ignition.  
5. Install wheel chocks.   
 
6. Disconnect the cable from the negative terminal.  
 
7. Care fully r emove the headliner  and associated components  to gain access to the spot welds  
at the front of the cab .
