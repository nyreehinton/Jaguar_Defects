V. BACKGROUND  
 
In the subject vehicles, the vapor leak detection monitor system may not detect a vapor leak from the fuel filler cap 
and illuminate the malfunction indicator lamp (MIL).  If fuel vapor leaks from the filler cap, it is possible that there could be a fuel s mell.
