If seat adjustments are unavailable, see symptoms below, follow advice under “Service” .
Symptoms:
• No response from the seat, when adjustments are made with the Seat Control Knob
• Operation of Seat Control Knob, does not bring up seat menu on the user interface screen
• Automatic seat adjustment does not activate when switching driver profiles

Technical Journal 37116.1.0SERVICE:
Inspect PSMD/PSMP electrical connections for any damages or loose connectors. After visual 
inspection, try unplugging and re-plugging the PSMD/PSMP connectors. 
If problem still occurs, replace PSMD or PSMP.
* Note: After the replacement of either module, it is important to calibrate the seats according to 
VIDA. (see attachment TJ-37116_1.pdf)
PSMD: Power Seat Module Driver
PSMP: Power Seat Module Passenger
