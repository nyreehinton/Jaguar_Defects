Involved Region or Country North America
ConditionSome customers may comment on one or more of the following conditions:
• No reverse
• No shifting past third gear
Some technicians may find P2732 (Transmission Control Solenoid Valve 6 Stuck Off)
set in the Transmission Control Module (TCM).
CauseThis condition may be caused by a mis-stamped spacer plate and worn check ball
internal of the control valve.
Correction Replace the spacer plate and check balls in the control valve.Subject:
