SYMPTOM:Customers may experience one or more of the following:
●The A/C and heater buttons Fig. 1  in the ICS do not function after 15 minutes following a cold ambient
temperature startup and a warning up of the ICS.
●Intermittent functioning of the other ICS buttons Fig. 1 .
NOTE :The redundant HVAC controls available from the radio screen still function, allowing the
driver to make any necessary HVAC adjustments.
CAUSE: ICS switches
