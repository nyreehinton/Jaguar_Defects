SITUA TION
 A crack may have formed in the upper left
corner of the trunk opening (circled in red),
where the quarter panel contacts the left
lower corner of the rear window and trunk
water channel. 
 
Illustration of the inward-facing metal shows
the general location of the possible crack,
though the area to be checked in the
Procedure is on the “back side”/outward-
facing surface. 
 
The PVC seam sealant applied to the water
channel may not suf ficiently seal the channel
in the presence of the crack, resulting in a
small water leak into the trunks interior . 
 
 The green dots represent the location of
spot welds. 
 
CORRECTION
1. Check the inner surface of the trunk metal/water channel for the presence of a crack or metal tear .

where the panel curves/bends (see photos).
 
If NO crack/tear is seen/found on
the panel, the vehicle can be
reassembled, and the campaign can
be closed
