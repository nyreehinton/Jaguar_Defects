SITUA TION
The driver ’s seat heater doesn’t work after the installation of a replacement seat harness part number (P/N)
52 10 7 414 701.
 
CAUSE
Seat harness P/N 52 10 7 414 701 applies to the both the front driver ’s and the front passenger ’s seat. 
 
This harness is designed to pass the seat heater wiring through the CIS seat occupancy seat mat, even
though the CIS seat occupancy seat mat is only installed on the passenger side front seat. 
 
When this seat harness part number is used on the driver's side, the 10-pin electrical connector ’s A125*1B
doesn't plug into anything.
 
CORRECTION
For the driver ’s seat, jump the wires to simulate the CIS seat mat wiring as described in this Service
Information Bulletin.
 
PROCEDURE
1. When installing replacement seat harness 52 10 7 414 701 in the driver's seat, the harness must be modified for
the seat heater to function. 
 
2. Two jumper wires must be installed, or the wires can be removed from the connector , spliced, soldered together
and protected with heat shrink tubing.
 
See attached wiring diagram B52 03 25 Attachment
 
a. Install a jumper between pins 9 and 10 connector A125*1B. 
b. Install a jumper between pins 7 and 8 connector A125*1B.
