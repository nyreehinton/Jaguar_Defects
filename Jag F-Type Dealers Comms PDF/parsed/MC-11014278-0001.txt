Units:  All Precedent  
Description:  Improved bolts  and flatwashers are now available to repair compressors found 
with broken flange bolts.  
  
Service Procedure:  
• If a broken flange bolts  are found inspect for excessive crankshaft radial play, if radial play is 
OK, replace bolts and flatwashers. Use Kit 200286.  
• If a compressor seal is being replaced install bolt update kit  
Note:  Compressor crankshaft radial play (up/down movement) should be checked on any 
compressor found with a broken flange bolt. Verify radial play is not excessive.
