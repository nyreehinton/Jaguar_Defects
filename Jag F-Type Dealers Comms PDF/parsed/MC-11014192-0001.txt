Introduction  
Some 2024 model year Tacoma Hybrid TRD Pro vehicles  equipped with a front seat suspension 
absorber assembly may exhibit a condition where any of the gauge lenses on the front seat 
suspension absorber assemblies may appear foggy . This condition may be present on either the 
driver or the front passenger seat. Follow the Repair Procedure in this bulletin to address  
this condition .
