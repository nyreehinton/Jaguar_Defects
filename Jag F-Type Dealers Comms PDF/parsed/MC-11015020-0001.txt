Air brake rework instructions.  
                  
1. Chock wheels  
2. Release parking brake  
3. Cage brakes using caging bolt and nut stored on brake chamber  
      
  
4. Apply parking brake to release air pressure  
5. Label air hoses as service or park  
6. Remove air hoses from brake chamber making note of parking/service brake fitting 
locations  
7. Remove ½” and ¼” clevis pins  
 
8. Unbolt and remove brake chamber  
9. Remove clevis and thread jam nut farther onto threaded rod  
10. Verify parking brake is fully caged, the threaded rod should measure 5.7 5” +/-.125  
Cage bolt storage location  
Insert cage bolt and rotate 
clockwise ¼ turn to engage 
slot  
½” clevis pin  
¼” clevis pin  
” clevis pin  
11. Mark threaded rod at 4 -3/8” from chamber mounting surface and cut.  (approximately 1 -
3/8” removed) , cut threaded rod and clean threads.  
 
12. Reinstall clevis making sure threaded  rod has less than 1/8”  sticking out of clevis and run 
down jam nut  
13. If needed because of a tight slack adjuster, remove snap ring, remove slack adjuster and 
rotate the top forward and reinstall adjuster and snap ring.  
 
14. Install chamber in top set of holes  
 
15. Reconnect air lines  
16. Turn 7/16” slack adjuster bolt to line up slack adjuster top large hole and clevis large hole 
and install ½” clevis and cotter pins  

 
17.  Install ¼” clevis and cotter pins  
18. Rotate tires to verify that there is no excessive drag on the brakes  
19. Repeat process for 3 other brake chambers  
20. Release parking brake and remove caging bolts and store them in the chamber slot  
21. Apply and fully release service brake 2 0 times to allow automatic slack adjusters to finish 
adjustment process
