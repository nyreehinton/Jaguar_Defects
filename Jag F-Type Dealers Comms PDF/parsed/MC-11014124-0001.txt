DESCRIPTION:
If a vehicle is reported with a complaint about a non-functioning interior deco backlight in the door, 
please follow advice under “Service” .

SERVICE:
1. Check the Connector:
• Examine the connectors of the Deco backlight. Refer to the attached images SE_37035_1.jpeg 
and SE_37035_2.jpeg for guidance.
2. Reconnect if Necessary:
• If any of the connectors are disconnected, reattach them securely. Use the instructions in the 
image SE_37035_2.jpeg for reference.
3. Secure Connection:
• Ensure the connector is firmly in place. If you are unable to secure it properly, proceed to the 
next step.
Page 2 of 4 2025-01-24
Technical Journal 37035.1.04. Inspect the Pins:
• Check the pins for any damage. Refer to the attached image SE-37035_3.jpeg for an 
example of damaged pins.
• If the pins are damaged, you will need to replace the Door Deco to rectify the issue.
5. Restore Connection:
• Once the connection is restored, the Deco backlight should function properly.
By following these steps, you can ensure the Deco backlight is connected and operational.
