Page 1 of 2DESCRIPTION:
A new electrical powertrain ERAD / TVDC has been launched in model year 2025.
During the first year of production of this new ERAD, we need to be aware of quality issues as 
quickly as possible.
For this reason, CMQ (Volvo Cars Central Quality Organization) must be contacted before 
performing any repairs on the ERAD/TVDC unit.
No ERAD/TVDC units shall be opened or replaced without approval or request from the CMQ.
ERAD = Electric Rear Axle Drive
TVDC = Torque Vectoring by Dual Clutch

SERVICE:
Please follow the information under Vehicle Report.
  
Warranty claim info:
Claim requests may be rejected unless a vehicle report is created.
Note that the TJ number must be stated in the repair order text.
VEHICLE REPORT:
Yes, please submit a Vehicle Report in all cases of ERAD/TVDC failure. Describe the customer’s 
symptoms and the dealer’s observation carefully in detail.
Use the concern area “Vehicle Report” and the sub-concern area “Support Needed” for 
issues that require fault tracing assistance or a hardware replacement of the ERAD/TVDC.
Use the sub-concern area “support Not Needed” for other issues, use function group 4632.
