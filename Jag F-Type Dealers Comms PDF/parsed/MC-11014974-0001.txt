Title: Next Gen Bus Top Step Tread Nosing Repair
Applies To: Next Gen Bus, PB11000 and PC11000
CHANGE LOG  
 
Please refer to the change log text box below for recent changes to this article:
 
02/28/2025 - Initial Article Release
 
 
DESCRIPTION
 
This document will guide the user through repairing a loose or raised top step nosing for Next Gen Bus. 
Note : There has been a design change to the top tread step nosing that minimizes the gaps on the forward side of the tread step nosing (see
below). A gap can be seen in the previous design that can be identified from outside the front door , looking towards the top step inside. Updated
Step Tread Part Numbers: 4630982C1(white), 4630983C1 (yellow).
 
 
 
SYMPT OM(s)
Customer Observations or Concerns:
Step nosing is loose, wavy , or does not sit flat to the step surface; see Figure 1 below . The gap can be seen from the driver's seat, looking towards
the top step tread nosing.
