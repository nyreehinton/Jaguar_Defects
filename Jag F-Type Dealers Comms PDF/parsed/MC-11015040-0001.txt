Subject:   
 
 
  Debris Tank Door 
Retention Clip Replacement Kit (VMV000097)
 
Background: DEBRIS TANK DOOR MAY FALL UNEXPECTEDLY 
Debris tank door lift cylinder retention clip can become displaced during 
operation and the tank door may fall.   
DEATH OR SERIOUS INJURY POSSIBLE 
If the debris tank door were to lower unexpectedly while cleaning the debris tank 
or underside of debris door, the operator or crew member could be crushed. 
Death or serious injury is possible.  
 
Solution: IMMEDIATE MACHINE MODIFICATIONS REQUIRED 
VMV00097 has been created to provide the necessary parts and instructions to update the retention clip. The kit must be installed as soon as possible.  
 Vermeer Corporation is administering this product safety alert on behalf of Vermeer MV Solutions, Inc.
