SITUA TION
Upon opening and closing the tailgate, a creaking/groaning sound can be heard from tailgate actuator . 
 
Note: Please see attached video 51 04 23.
 
CAUSE

about:blank 1/3
Excess friction caused by dirt/dust entry into the tailgate actuator and/or striker
 
CORRECTION
Clean and lubricate the tailgate actuator and striker .
 
PROCEDURE
Verify that a popping sound from the tailgate is heard. If yes, proceed to step 1.
1. Using a clean dry cloth, clean the tailgate actuator and striker. Compressed air can be used if
necessary. 
2. Using a small bush, lubricate the tailgate actuator and striker using special grease RHF 1.
3. Open and close the tailgate multiple times to spread the grease.
