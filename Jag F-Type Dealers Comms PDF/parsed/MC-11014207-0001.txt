Introduction  
Some 201 6 – 2018 model year  GS 450h , 2013 – 2016 model year LS 600h , and 2018 – 2021  
model year NX 300h  vehicles may exhibit a condition in which the brake, ABS, and/or TRAC warning 
lights are on and/or a Check VSC message displays. One or more of the following specific Diagnostic 
Trouble C odes (DTCs) C1391, C1252, C1256, and/or C1253 will be stored in the vehicle memory to 
be used as verification. This condition may be caused by a small internal brake fluid leak inside the 
brake actuator. Follow the Repair Procedure in this bulletin to addr ess this condition.

Repair Procedure  
1. Confirm  the condition exists.  
Are the brake, ABS, and/or TRAC warning lights ON and/or does a Check VSC  
message display?  
 YES — Continue to step 2.  
 NO — This bulletin does NOT apply. Continue diagnosis using the applicable  
 Repair Manual.  
 
 
2. Using  GTS+ , check for and record ANY stored DTCs.  
Are ANY of the following DTCs stored: C1391, C1252, C1256, and/or C1253?  
 YES — Continue to step 3.  
 NO — This bulletin does NOT apply. Continue diagnosis using the applicable  
 Repair Manual.  
 
 
3. Remove the brake  actuator  and brake booster  assembly  w/ master cylinder . 
Refer to TIS, applicable model and model year Repair Manual:  
 2013  / 2014  / 2015  / 2016  LS 600h :  
Brake – Brake Control – Brake Actuator  – Removal  
 
 2016 – 2018 GS 450h : 
Brake – Brake Control / Dynamic Control Systems  – Brake Actuator  – Removal  
 2018 – 2020  / 2020 – 2021  NX 300h : 
Brake – Brake System  (Other)  – Brake Booster  – Removal  
 
 
4. Is the vehicle an NX 300h, GS 450h , or an LS 600h ? 
 If an NX 300h continue to step 5.  
 If a GS 450h or LS 600h  go to step 6.  
 
   NOTE  
Procedure includes removal of the brake accumulator pump assembly .

Repair Procedure  (continued)  
5. Remove the brake booster pump assembly.  
Refer to TIS, applicable model and model year Repair Manual:  
 2018 – 2020 / 2021  NX 300h : 
Brake – Brake System (Other) – Brake Booster Pump  – Removal  
 
 
6. Install the NEW brake actuator and brake booster assembly w/ master cylinder . 
Refer to TIS, applicable model and model year Repair Manual:  
 2013  / 2014  / 2015  / 2016  LS 600h : 
Brake – Brake Control – Brake Actuator  – Installation  
 
 2016 – 2018  GS 450h : 
Brake – Brake Control / Dynamic Control Systems  – Brake Actuator  – Installation  
 2018 – 2020 / 2020 – 2021  NX 300h : 
Brake – Brake System  (Other)  – Brake Booster  – Installation  
 
 
7. Is the vehicle an NX 300h, GS 450h , or an LS 600h ? 
 If an NX 300h continue to step 8.  
 If a GS 450h or LS600h  go to step 9.  
 
 
8. Install the NEW brake booster pump assembly.  
Refer to TIS, applicable model and model year Repair Manual:  
 2018 – 2020  / 2020 – 2021  NX 300h : 
Brake – Brake System (Other) – Brake Booster Pump  – Installation  
 
 
9. Using GTS+ , clear ANY stored DTCs and test -drive the vehicle to confirm the conditions  
are no longer present.  
 
 
 NOTE  
Procedure includes installation  of the brake accumulator pump assembly .
