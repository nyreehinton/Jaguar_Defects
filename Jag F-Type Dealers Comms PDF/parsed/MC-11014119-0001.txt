Please start by following this journal prior to any replacements:
• If the left rear turn indicator is not working, continue the fault tracing and repair according to our 
VIDA instructions.
• If the left rear turn indicator is working, through visual inspection and DTC stated above is set, 
do not replace any parts.

31543.
