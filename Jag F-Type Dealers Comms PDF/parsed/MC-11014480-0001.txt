REPAIR SUMMARY:
This bulletin provides information regarding the latest Firmware Over The Air (FOTA) update and provides the
service technician an overview of the steps a customer will need to take to complete the update. The radio
software will be updated from T27.52 to U29.44 then U33.43.
NOTE: The national launch for the FOTA is expected to begin December 08, 2024.
DISCUSSION:
**The FOTA Campaign has been put on hold until further notice.**
