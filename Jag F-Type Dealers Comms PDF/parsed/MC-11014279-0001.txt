Involved Region or Country North America, Middle East
ConditionSome customers may comment on the display is hot to the touch when temperatures
outside are warmer or directly in sunlight.
Screen is operating as designed but will feel hot to the touch if the outside temperature is
higher than 77 degrees and vehicle is in the sun.
CauseThe cause of the condition may be due to the outside temperature and/or the sun hitting
the screen is making the screen hot to the touch.
