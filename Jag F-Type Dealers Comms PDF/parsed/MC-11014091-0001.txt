AUX HEAT FUEL DRAIN PLUG  RETROFIT  
Retrofit Description:  
This procedure replaces the drain plug on the aux heat fuel tank.

Procedure  
1. Complete the Proterra approved Lockout/Tagout procedure to make the bus safe for work.  
 
2. Using a Square Key, open the rear hatch to access the a uxiliary  heat fuel tank.  
 
 
3. Place a Catch Pan underneath the auxiliary heat fuel tank to contain the fuel in the tank.  
 
4. Using a 3/8- Inch Combination Wrench, remove the drain plug and discard it.  
  
 
5. Allow all the fuel to drain into the catch pan.  
 
6. Apply Loctite 567 to the threads on the new Plug (050926).  
 
7. Using a 3/8- Inch Combination Wrench, install the new Plug (050926) into the tank.  
 
8. Torque the new Plug (050926) one and one half to three turns past finger tight.  
 
9. Fill the tank with diesel fuel.  
 
10. Using a Square Key, close and latch the rear hatch.
