SITUA TION
A squeaking sound is heard when folding either of the exterior mirrors assemblies in or out.
 
Note: Please see attached video V51 02 24.
 
CAUSE
Unfavorable tolerance positions within the exterior mirror assembly .
 
CORRECTION
Clean and lubricate the sliding surface of the exterior mirror assembly .
 
PROCEDURE
1. Verify that the customer ’s vehicle has this issue. 
2. Manually fold the exterior mirror assembly inwards (see photo 1 in the attachment). 
3. Clean the sliding surface on the exterior mirror assembly and apply PF1 lubricant (see photo 2 in the attachment)
4. Manually fold the exterior mirror assembly outwards (see photo 3 in the attachment).
5. Clean the sliding surface on the exterior mirror assembly and apply PFI lubricant (see photo 4 in the attachment). 
6. If the sound is still present, replace the af fected exterior mirror assembly .
