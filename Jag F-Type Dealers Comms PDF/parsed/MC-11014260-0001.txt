Title:  BL1075/BL1076 update
Applicability: Revero GT and GTS, GS-6 vehicles  
Market Region:  All                                                                                      Type: Technical Service Bulletin
Year: 2025
System: Infortainment (94)
Sequence: 01PRIORITY:  All
PURPOSE:  A software update for Karma Vehicles to connect to the current servers for future over-the-air -updates
Vehicles in the field will all receive this update over the air. Should this update not be accepted, create  
a TSS case and follow the representative guidance to update the vehicle manually. The software 
installed for the CIU is K1-21-1075.RC1 for Revero GT and GS-6  and K1-21-1076.RC1 for Revero 
GTS/ GS-6 Sport motor variants.
When the prompt is received accept the download and wait for the vehicle to complete the process.
The new CIU ID should now read 21.35.52.2.
