Instructions  for Valve Stem Extension  Recall 24V 787  
Inspect the rear wheels to see if  valve stem extensions are installed on your coach.  
If valve stem extensions are installed , take picture s of each wheel . 
Then submit your information and the photos via Newmar’s recall 
form . If valve stem extensions are installed, proceed with the following 
recall instructions to remove the valve stem extension(s). T here is no 
need to take the coach  to a dealership to have the recall completed . 
Determine if you are willing to complete the recall yourself or would 
like a dealer to complete  the recall  for you. If you do not want to 
complete the recall yourself, contact a Newmar -authorized dealer or 
service center to schedule the recall.  
If performing the recall yourself : 
1. Remove the valve stem cap . 
2. Remove the rubber grommet from the outer rim . 
3. Remove the valve stem extension from the rear inn er dual tire  by rotating the 
extension counterclockwise . 
4. Test and adjust the rear in ner tire pressure to ensure correct air pressure and that air 
loss did not occur while removing the extension.  This can be checked  using  a long, 
truck -style tire pressure gauge and a long , truck -style air chuck . 
5. Install the valve stem cap you previously removed  using  long nose pliers or a valve 
capper tool . 
6. Take a photo of  the rear tire and rim  showing the extension and grommet have been 
removed.  
7. Scan the QR code or use web link below  to ﬁ ll out the recall form . Remember to 
attach the  pictures you took to verify the recall has been completed.
