Description:  Certain 2024MY Santa Fe  Hybrid  (MX5A  HEV) vehicles  may experience multiple  alerts 
for In-Cabin C amera (ICC) blockage due to driving with hand (s) at the 12 o’clock position.  Updating the 
ICC logic reduces the number of alerts . 
 
Applicable Vehicles  (Certain) : 
• 2024MY Santa Fe Hybrid (MX5A HEV) produced from 03/06/2024–10/09/2024
