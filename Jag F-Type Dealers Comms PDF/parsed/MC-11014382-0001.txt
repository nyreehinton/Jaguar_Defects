SYMPTOM:Customers may experience the following:
●One or both front doors will not open or close properly using the exterior door handle
NOTE :This symptom can be more noticeable due to icy conditions.
CAUSE: Manufacturing issue
DISCUSSION:
This Information Only TSB is to communicate the updated parts numbers for the exterior door handle support
mechanism Fig. 1  and cable.
Fig. 1
Exterior Door Handle Support Mechanism
1 - Old Design
2 - Updated Design

NOTE: ● If the driver door is ajar, an audible warning and two visual indications are provided on
the Instrument Panel Cluster (IPC) to notify the driver the door is not closed. Additionally
the parking brake will apply when attempting to drive away if the driver door is not latched
close.
● If the passenger door is ajar, an audible warning and two visual indications are provided to
notify to the driver the door is not latched.
● The issue occurs only in severe low temperature conditions and when vehicle’s interior
temperature increases the behavior is restored.
