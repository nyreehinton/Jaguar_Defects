DIAGNOSIS:
If a customer’s VIN is listed in VIP or your RSU VIN list, perform the repair. If any vehicle not on the VIN list
exhibits any of the symptom listed above in the customer symptom section, perform the Repair Procedure.
REPAIR PROCEDURE:
1. Open the hood.
2. Check the gap between the DRL brackets and hood with a piece of paper.
3. Is there contact between the piece of paper and the hood at the mounting points Fig. 1 ?
Fig. 1
Checking Gap
● YES>>> Proceed to Step 4 .
● NO>>> This bulletin does not apply. Use Inspection LOP (08-50-65-96). Perform further diagnostics.
4. Remove the DRL. Refer to the detailed service procedures listed in DealerCONNECT>Service
Library>Service Info under: 5540B11 - LED Side Lights (DRL) Front Light Cluster One, LH OR RH - R.R.
5. Using a light, inspect carefully the mounting areas of the DRL.
6. Are there cracks visible on and near the mounting points?
● YES>>> Proceed to Step 8 .
● NO>>> Proceed to Step 7 .
7. Re-install the DRL by inserting two 2.5mm (.010 in.) spacers between the DRL mount and the hood.
Re-install fasteners and push pins.B23 FSAR-RSU-4883 -2-
8. Replace the DRL. Refer to the detailed service procedures listed in DealerCONNECT>Service
Library>Service Info under: 5540B11 - LED Side Lights (DRL) Front Light Cluster One, LH OR RH - R.R.
