Introduction  
Some 2021 – 2024 model year Sienna Hybrid  vehicles may exhibit a condition in which the front 
passenger carpet is untucked and torn at the center console , as shown in Figure 1. To address 
this concern, a carpet patch  has been developed to reduce the likelihood of this condition from 
occurring.  Follow the Repair Procedure in this bulletin to address this condition.
