Service Procedure
1. Confirm condition is present, “Settings, Vehicle, Audio Settings, Sound, Sound Mode”. If menu opens, issue is
not related to this bulletin. If Sound Mode menu opens momentarily, goes blank, and the audio settings menu
opens, proceed to step 2.
2. Contact the Techline Customer Support Center (TCSC) for related VCI number programming assistance.
Reference Bulletin # 25–NA-036.
3. Connect a USB-C USB Drive to the computer, perform a quick format on the usb drive to NTFS.
4. Launch TLC/SPS2
