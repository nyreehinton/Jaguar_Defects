 Service Procedure : 
IMPORTANT:  Follow all general safety procedures and PPE guidelines – diesel fuel can be 
hazardous.  
1. Turn the unit OFF using the HMI.  
2. Open the front engine access doors , the roadside service panel , and the top door.  
3. Set the Service / Microprocessor Switch to the OFF position.  
4. Locate the  fuel filter head mounted above the engine, near the air filter housing.   
5. Inspect the two  1/4 inch fuel lines from the filter to the engine and the  one 3/8 inch fuel  return 
line for any signs of chafing, rubbing, leakage or other distress. See image below.  
6. Replace the fuel lines if any signs of chafing, rubbing, leakage or other distress  exists.

 7. Secure The Fuel Lines:   
Because of variability in how the units were installed, some units may require one cable tie, 
and other units  may require two cable ties . The cable ties  should be located approximately 6 
inches and 9 inches below the filter fitting .  
 
Note: If a fuel line required replacement, also ensure that a third cable  tie (near the injection 
pump) is reinstalled .  
 
 
8. Set the Service / Microprocessor Switch to the ON position.  
9. Verify proper unit operation and check for fuel leaks.  
10. Close the panels and doors.  
11. Place a claim in TAVANT for this campaign , see page 4.
