OVERVIEWTT-25-001 Front Hub Tone Ring Identification
1Subject vehicles may require a front axle hub to be replace for a variety of reasons. 
The following information will provide guidance for purchasing the correct hub. Installation of the wrong hub will result in ABS wheel speed codes .  
SUBJECT VEHICLES
2016 model  year  Hino  conventional  vehicles  to current  model  year .
Hub Options
There  are two different front axle hubs  available for purchase:
1)100 tooth wheel  speed tone wheel  (Standard Conventional ; 22.5”  Tire)
2)80 tooth wheel  speed tone wheel  (Low  Profile Conventional ; 19.5”  Tire)
Note: Some  Standard Conventional  truck’s  may have been converted into a Low-
Profile truck . In this situation you would need to install  the 100-tooth hub. If you 
are not sure what  hub to purchase; count the teeth on the hub being replaced to 
confirm  the correct  hub is  ordered and installed.
