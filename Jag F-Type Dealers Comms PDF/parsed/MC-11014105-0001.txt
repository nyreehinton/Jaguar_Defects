SERVICE:
This could be a CEM (Central Electronic Module) related issue.
Do not replace any parts prior to performing the fix/workaround as stated below.
As a temporary fix/workaround (and please inform customer): 
1. Use key fob button
2. Lock the car and unlock again
3. No need to let the car go in to deep sleep
