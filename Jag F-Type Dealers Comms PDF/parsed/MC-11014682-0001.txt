Title  Sunroof Waterdrain – Water Ingress  
Affected Vehicle(s)  Grenadier  PF00001  -  
This Technical Service Bulletin (TSB) should only apply to vehicles that demonstrate the described issue 
below.  
 
Reason for this document  
Issue:  An issue has been identified on a small percentage of the affected vehicles where water marks 
appear on the roofliner around the A -Pillar area and water accumulation in  the footwell . 
Cause:  Water Accumulation around the drain pipe within the sunroof aperture or panel . 
Action:  Follow the procedure below.
