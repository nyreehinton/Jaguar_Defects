SUBJECT:  KEY FOB  INSPECTION/ REPLACEMENT  
(SERVICE CAMPAIGN  9B9) 
 
 
Description:  Certain IONIQ 5 (NE A EV) vehicles may have come with the incorrect key fob  for the trim 
level. Follow the procedure in this bulletin to inspect and, if necessary, replace  with the correct key fob . 
 
  
 
Applicable Vehicles  (Certain) : 
• 2025MY IONIQ 5 (NEA EV) produced from 12/10/2024 – 01/24/2025  
 
 
 
 
 
 
Dealers must perform this service campaign on all affected vehicles prior to customer retail delivery and whenever an affected vehicle is in the shop for any maintenance or repair.  
 Access the “Vehicle Information” screen via W ebDCS to identify open campaigns.  
 IMPORTANT  
To avoid  any potential damage to IONIQ vehicles , this service campaign can only be performed 
at IONIQ certified dealers.
