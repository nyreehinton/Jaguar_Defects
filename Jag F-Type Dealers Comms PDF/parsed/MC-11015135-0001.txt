ENGINE
The 2.0L MIVEC (Mitsubishi Innovative Valve-timing Electronic Control) engine balances  
maximum HP of 148 with low fuel consumption for a smooth, efficient drive.  The SEL 
model features even more power with a 2.4L version of the MIVEC.
TRANSMISSION/DRIVE SYSTEM
All models equipped with All Wheel Control (AWC) have 4WD control logic that protects 
the drive-train  by suppressing heat generation of the electronic-control coupling and 
provides extraordinary traction and stability in all driving conditions.
CONVENIENCE/TECHNOLOGY
8" Smartphone Link Display Audio system is a standard feature beginning with the LE 
model.  
Mitsubishi's advanced Smartphone Link Display Audio system works seamlessly with 
Apple CarPlay while available Android Auto infuses convenience and enjoyment into 
every drive.

Rear Seat Alert
Rear seat alert is activated when there is potential for leaving an occupant in the rear seat 
when the vehicle is placed in park.
•System works based on the opening and closing of the rear door.
•System will flash "Check Rear Seat" symbol along with an audible warning.

Active Stability Control (ASC)
Active Stability Control applies braking force to all wheels as needed, utilizing the Anti-
Lock Braking System, traction control, and skid control for
 a reliable driving experience 
in any environment.
SAFETY FEATURES 
7-Airbag System
The Outlander Sport includes six standard airbags:  front, front-seat-mounted, and side-
curtain airbags.  There is also a seventh airbag found
 underneath the driver's side 
instrument panel.
Rain Sensing Wipers
Rain Sensing Windshield Wipers activate at
the first drop of rain and adjust to match the rainfall.
RISE Body Construction
Reinforced Impact Safety Evolution (RISE) technology helps to absorb and redirect 
impacts in the event of a collision.  Strategically-placed high-tensile strength steel 
assists in minimizing cabin deformation, maintains cabin integrity, and helps protect 
passengers against side impacts.

Lane Departure Warning (LDW)
LDW system regularly monitors your lane position and give audible and visual alerts if it 
notices an unintentional lane departure.
Automatic High Beam (AHB)
AHB will automatically switch between high and low headlights to reduce glare for other 
drivers if other vehicles ahead of you or in oncoming lanes are detected.
SAFETY FEATURES (Continued)
Forward Collision Mitigation (FCM) with Pedestrian Detection
FCM detects likely frontal collisions and warns the driver with audible and visual alerts.  
Brakes are applied to help avoid or reduce the severity of the collision.
