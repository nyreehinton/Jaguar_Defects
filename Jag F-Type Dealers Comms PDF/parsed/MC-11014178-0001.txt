Introduction  
Some 2019  – 2024 model year 4Runner vehicles may exhibit a cracking noise from the driver  
and/or passenger front seat cushion track. This condition may occur when the front seat cushion 
track is at the rearmost position. Follow the Repair Procedure in this bulletin to address 
this condition .

Repair Procedure  
1. Confirm the condition exists.  
Does the vehicle exhibit a cracking noise from the driver and/or passenger front seat 
cushion  track ? This condition may occur when the front seat cushion track is at the  
rearmost position . 
• YES — Continue to step 2.  
• NO — This bulletin does NOT apply. Continue diagno sis using the applicable  
 Repair  Manual.  
 
 
2. Move the front seat with the condition to the frontmost position . 
 
 
3. Confirm which seat configuration the vehicle has equipped and proceed to the 
appropriate  step.  
• If the vehicle is equipped with a manual seat configuration, continue to step 4. 
• If the vehicle is equipped with a power seat configuration, go to step 5. 
 
 
4. Remove the front manual seat.  
Refer to TIS, applicable model and model year Repair Manual:  
• 2019 – 2024  4Runner : 
Vehicle Interior – Seat – Front Seat Assembly (for Manual Seat) – Removal  
• Go to Step 6.  
 
 
5. Remove the front power seat. 
Refer to TIS, applicable model and model year Repair Manual:  
• 2019 – 2024  4Runner : 
Vehicle Interior – Seat – Front Seat Assembly (for Power  Seat) – Removal  
• Continue to Step 6.
