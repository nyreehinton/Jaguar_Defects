SITUA TION
 Due to changes in the opening mechanism of
the trunk/tailgate latch, it is possible that the
trunk/tailgate may not completely lock after
closing it.
 
CORRECTION
Replace the trunk/tailgate latch.
 
PROCEDURE
Replace the latch that secures the trunk/tailgate following the repair instructions listed in IST A/AIR 51 24
100.
