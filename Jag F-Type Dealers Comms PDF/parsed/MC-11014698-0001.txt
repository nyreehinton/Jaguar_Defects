WHAT IS THE ISSUE?  
The brake hydraulic control unit could fail. Over time, this could cause the antilock braking system (ABS), electronic stability control (ESC) system and/or traction control not to work. An antilock braking system, electronic stability control 
system and/or traction control system that doesn't work could increase the risk of a crash.
