 
 
SERVICE ADVISORY # 25-461 
Coleman Cooktop 
It has been determined that the cooktop may become dislodged from its opening. The 
following procedure describes how to secure cooktop in the opening. 
Models Included: Coleman Model 13B Travel Trailer  
Serial Number Range:  SH939648 - SH940789  
 
Note – Check serial number on Key Express to verify the campaign is open. Some vehicles were 
repaired before they left Keystone. 
Parts Required:  1 Yard – KRV # 110228 - Velcro - Hook - 1" - Black 
1 Yard – KRV # 110229 - Velcro - Loop - 1" - Black 
Tools Required:  Staple Gun 
When performing warranty or recall service, please make certain that appropriate Personal 
Protective Equipment (PPE) is used. 
The remedy is to install Velcro in the cooktop opening and to the base of cooktop to secure cooktop for 
travel. 
REPAIR INSTRUCTIONS  
Step 1 
Locate the trailer on a level, flat, hard surface. Chock the wheels. Disconnect all power. 
Step 2  
Inspect cooktop opening 
for installed Velcro to 
hold cooktop in place. If 
Velcro is installed on 
opening and cooktop as 
shown in Figures 1 and 
2, inspection is complete.
