A. COMPLIMENTARY UPGRADE C000017 DESCRIPTION
Volvo Car USA LLC and Volvo Car Canada LTD, on behalf of Volvo Car Corporation, has decided 
to launch Complimentary Upgrade – C000017 on certain Android-based Infotainment system  
(iCup) vehicles that have missed three (3) or more consecutive Over-The-Air (OTA) downloads.
C000017 also addresses cer tain EX90s that have missed one (1) or more OTA downloads.
Complimentary Upgrade C000017 addresses vehicles that are no longer able to receiv e OTA 
updates. This Upgrade is necessary to have the correct baseline for future OTA deplo yments. In 
order to explain the features and benefi ts we recommend noting the software level before the 
download is completed. This will aid in explaining to the customer what improvements have 
been added since their last software download.
To remedy concerned cars, please follow the instructions outlined in TJ 36139.
Complimentary Upgrade C000017 includes some of following software updates:
They can also be found here:
https://www.volvocars.com/lb/support/software-release-notes
SPA, Software Version 3.3.16
Android Auto can be used with Android devices via the center display, your Android phone or 
with the right-side steering wheel buttons. When installed, it lets you listen to music, make 
calls, get driving directions and use apps customized for your car. To use the Android Auto app, 
it needs to be installed on your Android device and connected to the internet.

B. VEHICLES INVOLVED
NOTE:  RETAILER MUST CONFIRM VEHICLE ELIGIBILITY PRIOR TO BEGINNING 
THE REPAIR FOR THIS COMPLIMENTARY UPGRADE.
Vehicle eligibility must be confirmed:
• Vehicle Inquiry – Warranty Vehicle Inquiry where the message “Complimentary Upgrade 
C000017” will appear for eligible vehicles, F4=History from the main Inquiry menu must 
be selected to confirm Complimentary Upgrade C000017 has not been completed. 
Eligibility can also be confirmed in TIE. 
All vehicles must be checked for any incomplete recalls or service campaigns or service 
upgrades. All open Recall, Service Campaign or Service Action repairs must be completed.
If you have any questions concerning this recall, send them to recall@volvocars.com. 
C.  PORT VEHICLES
Vehicles marked for Complimentary Upgrade C000017 will not be done at the port. It is the 
retailer’s responsibility to perform on eligible vehicles. Pre-Delivery Software (PDS) will satisfy 
the requirements under Complimentary Upgrade C000017.  
D. PARTS / PARTS RETURN 
No parts are required to be returned for this Complimentary Upgrade.
E.  OWNER NOTIFICATION
No owner notification for C000017.

Quality Bulletin C000017F .  VEHICLES IN RETAILER INVENTORY
New Vehicles in Retailer Inventory
Vehicles marked for Complimentary Upgrade C000017 that have not been retail delivered 
should install Pre-Delivery Software (PDS). The PDS software will satisfy the requirements of 
C000017.
Used Vehicles in Retailer Inventory
Please check Vehicle Inquiry, TIE, or VIDA for vehicle eligibility. 
G.  RETAILER RESPONSIBILITY
All vehicles qualifying for this Complimentary Upgrade must be repaired prior to a customer 
taking possession of the vehicle at new car delivery and or at any service appointment.
H. TECHNICIAN COMPETENCY REQUIREMENT 
The technician competency requirement for this campaign repair is Quality/G0.
