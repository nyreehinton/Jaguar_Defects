Interior Lamps / Illumination
All interior lamps have been upgraded to LED.  The map lamp, room lamp, and personal lamp 
can be turned on and off with a capacitive switch.  With the operation of the main switch 
and individual lamp switches, the lights now gradually turn on and off.  A foot lamp has been 
added to provide ground illumination when getting in and out of the car at night  (not 
standard).

Ventilated Front Seats
Front seat comfort has been enhanced by adding a 3-level adjustable ventilation feature to the 
semi-aniline leather / leather seats (SEL and SEL Premium only).  The system pulls air from 
HVAC ducts, has separate adjustable airflow levels for driver and passenger , and is adjustable 
in 3 stages to maintain a comfortable ride.
Frameless Digital Rearview Mirror with HomeLink ®
Exclusively available on SEL Premium and Platinum Edition , Outlander adopts a frameless 
design for the digital rear-view mirror.  The video from the camera installed at the rear of the 
vehicle is displayed on the rear-view camera.  You can turn the camera image on/off by 
moving the lever located in the center of the mirror forward and backward. 
Cup Holder
With the cup holder now located beside the shift lever instead of in the front armrest, it's easy to 
remove cups and  bottles, accommodating two 44-ounce cups.  Cup holders are tilted away 
from each other to prevent contact of the cups.
