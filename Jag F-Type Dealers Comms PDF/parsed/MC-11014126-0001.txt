Note: 
Please follow the CSC fault trace in VIDA to define the lock status of the HVCH.
There may be three different HVCH statuses:
• Locked until Next Start (follow VIDA fault trace)
• Locked until Service (follow VIDA fault trace)
• Locked permanently (proceed with replacement of the HVCH)
If the Permanent DTC-U133A93 and VIDA HVCH status is confirmed as permanently locked 
according to the component test, proceed with the replacement of the HVCH according to VIDA.
*Note: Ensure additional Heater is turned on in the Center Screen Display (CSD), If this is 
turned off you will have no heat pure mode.
