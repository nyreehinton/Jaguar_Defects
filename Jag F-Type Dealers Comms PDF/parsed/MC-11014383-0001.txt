SYMPTOM:**Customers may experience one or more of the following:**
●**If the driver door is ajar, an audible warning and two visual indications are provided on the Instrument
Panel Cluster (IPC) to notify the driver the door is not closed. Additionally the parking brake will apply when
attempting to drive away if the driver door is not latched close.
●If the passenger door is ajar, an audible warning and two visual indications are provided to notify to the
driver the door is not latched.
●The issue occurs only in severe low temperature conditions and when vehicle’s interior temperature
increases the behavior is restored.**
●One or both front doors will not open or close properly using the exterior door handle
NOTE :This symptoms can be more noticeable due to icy conditions.
CAUSE: Manufacturing issue
This bulletin supersedes Technical Service Bulletin (TSB) 23-005-25, date of issue February 11, 2025,
