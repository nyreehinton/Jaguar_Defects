   
SSM 53374   2025 Explorer/Aviator - Special Service Tool (SST) For GWM
Replacement
Some 2025 Explorer/Aviator vehicles may exhibit a concern where it would require gateway module (GWM)
replacement. The SST 419-001 socket called out in Workshop Manual (WSM) is currently not available to
dealers. The tool is available through the loaner program through the Rotunda Technician Tool Program
(RTTP). Contact the RTTP by calling the phone number on the Professional Technician System (PTS) website,
under the Rotunda tab. For dealers in the United States, select Option #3, for Canada, select Option #5.
