Introduction  
Some 2024  – 2025 model year Tacoma and Tacoma Hybrid vehicles  equipped with an accessory 
roof rack  may exhibit a n abnormal  vibration  from the accessory roof rack while drivin g. New roof 
rack bumper s have been developed to addres s this vibration . Follow the Repair Procedure in this 
bulletin to address  this condition.
